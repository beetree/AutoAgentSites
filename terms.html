Service

DFW Scrap Rates is a daily price-reporting service. Each weekday morning, we call a set of public-facing Dallas–Fort Worth scrap yards, capture the prices they give us, and summarize the top-paying yard for the copper grades we track. We deliver that summary by email and (for opt-in subscribers) by SMS.

Accuracy of prices

Prices we report are captured from yard staff during morning calls and are subject to change throughout the day. They are intended as a directional summary, not a quote. Actual payout you receive at any yard depends on the yard's policies, material quality, weight, and other factors at the time of sale.
