Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-02

DFW Scrap Rates ("we", "us") is a daily price-reporting service operated under Leet Group. This policy describes what information we collect from people who sign up for our email or SMS updates, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What we collect

  • Your name and business name (so we can address messages personally).
  • Your email address (to deliver the full daily DFW copper price report).
  • Your mobile phone number (only if you sign up for SMS updates).
  • Whether you opt in to SMS updates and the date/time of that opt-in.
  • Basic engagement signals such as whether you click links inside the daily report.

How we use it

  • To send you the daily DFW copper price summary you signed up for.
  • To respond to questions you send to us.
  • To improve which DFW scrap yards we cover and which copper grades we track.

We do not sell your information. We do not share your phone number, email, or opt-in status with third parties for marketing.

SMS specifics

If you opt in to SMS updates, you will receive up to approximately 5 messages per week (one per weekday morning) from DFW Scrap Rates. Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. Message frequency varies.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to DFW Scrap Rates from your enrolled number. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from this service. You can request help by replying HELP or by emailing info@leetgroup.net.

Data sharing

We use third-party service providers to deliver email and SMS messages and to host this website. Those providers process your information solely to deliver the messages on our behalf. We do not sell, rent, or trade your contact information.

Retention

We keep your contact information and opt-in record for as long as your subscription is active. If you opt out, we keep a minimal record of the opt-out (your number plus the date) so we do not message you again, but we stop using your information for any other purpose.

Contact

Questions about this policy or about your information: info@leetgroup.net.
